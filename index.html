Waterfall Slide in Shenadoah National Park

Jessup Campu and Jasmine Hinz went on the 2 mile hike in Shenadoah National Park to the waterfalls where they slide on the rocks into pools of refreshingly cold water. This is their first adventure outside of the Norfolk metropolitan area since they moved to VA from MN in Aug. 2022. Jasmine is a Marine Biology student at ODU and Jessup has been working to help in the relocation and successful completion of Jasmine's education.
